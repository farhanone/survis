This is a collection of scientific publications that present or discuss the mathematical foundations and properties of comparative measures for topological descriptors of scalar field and their use in real-world visualization applications along with their implementation.

The associated state-of-the-art report on scalar field comparison with topological descriptors will be published at EuroVis 2021.

Title

Scalar Field Comparison with Topological Descriptors : Properties and Applications for Scientific Visualization
